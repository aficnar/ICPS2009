A popular part of past ICPS conferences have been the scientific excursions. These are a selection of site visits to areas and facilities of scientific interest in and around the host city and are designed to give visiting delegates the opportunity to experience part of the country's scientific community. All have a scientific or engineering theme, and many also have a substantial historical or cultural element.

As soon as we prepare a list of possible excursions to choose from, they will be posted here.
