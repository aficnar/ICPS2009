Accommodation for participants of ICPS 2009 will be located at the "Učenički dom Split" dormitory in Split, Matice Hrvatske 13.

Please note that you will be able to arrange your roommates preference upon your registration in Split.
  • "Učenički dom Split" dormitory
    Accommodation for all ICPS2009 participants will be located at "Učenički dom Split" which is only 5 minutes away from FESB (our conference venue) on foot (address: Matice Hrvatske 13, Split). All the meals during the conference will be provided there and all the parties will be held there as well.
    The dormitory is a recently renovated nine storey building with an elevator and parking. All rooms have three beds and there are two (M/F) restrooms and showers on each floor. There are also several hostel-type chillout rooms with TVs. A wireless internet connection is available from the nearby faculties.
